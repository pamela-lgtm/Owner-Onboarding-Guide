Showings · Feedback · Applications

Vacancy management — weekly cadence, market-based adjustments, qualified applicant to owner
14–45 Days 0 of 0
Role split in this phase: Abril processes applications and makes the approval/denial decision based on Verandah's rental standards — the owner does not review or decide on individual applicants. Linda is the communication bridge: she receives updates from Abril on application status and relays them to the owner. The owner hears from Linda only, consistent with the white-glove single-point-of-contact experience.
Decision Branches · HOA
  • HOA tenant packet required?If yes, packet + admin fee coordinated before lease execution (e.g., Laureate Park $200).
Linda's Cadence
Weekly Owner Updates are Linda's rhythm — she keeps the owner close through the vacancy with a standing cadence of calls and text/email updates (roughly 3-day, then 7-day intervals), adjusting to the owner's communication preferences. This is hand-held, not scheduled in a task list. Athenee can mirror the cadence as a ClickUp recurring task if helpful for visibility, but the relationship lives with Linda.
